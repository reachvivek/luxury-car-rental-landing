HOW IT WORKS

Renting your perfect vehicle is simple and hassle-free. Follow these three easy steps to get behind the wheel of your dream car.

1

Contact & Select

Contact us via WhatsApp or call to browse our collection. Choose your preferred vehicle and select your desired time slot for pickup.

2

Book & Pay

Pay the booking amount upfront. For luxury cars, a security deposit is required to ensure vehicle safety. All payments are secure and transparent.

3

Pickup & Drive

Arrive at the pickup location at your scheduled time. Complete a quick vehicle inspection and you're ready to drive away smoothly!

Security deposits apply only to luxury vehicles and are fully refundable upon safe return
